Process over Product

The 'process over product concept' was something I really struggled with for this sprint essentially because it meant finishing my site when I wasn't 100% happy with it. The inner perfectionish in me revolted against this pretty hard, and I ended up going over my allocated time. This is something I really need to work on, as it is not always possible to launch a project with it being 100% perfect.


Inital ideas on Meditation

Before reading chapter 2 of 'Search Inside Yourself' I had some knowledge of meditation, having been using the Headspace App off and on for a few years. However, I've never managed to make a lasting regular practice of it, and I'm hoping the meditation work I'm doing on this course will help me achieve that.


Meditation Learnings

Chapter 2 of 'Search Inside Yourself' talks more about the practise of mindful meditation, and the theory behind it. There are several key points in this chapter that I found really interesting, the first being that meditation is like exercise for the brain, and can be practised and improved on over time, just like physical exercise. I also loved the concept of 'intention creating' - that if you create the same intention over and over, it essentially becomes a reality for you.

Meditation Techniques

I am still using the Headspace App for my meditation practice as I find it a good way to guide my practise, but I also like the technique they discuss in this chapter on how to deal with distractions:

  1. Acknowledge.
  2. Experience without judging or reacting.
  3. If you need to react, continue maintaining mindfulness while doing so, using intention, movement, and sensation.
  4. Let it go.
